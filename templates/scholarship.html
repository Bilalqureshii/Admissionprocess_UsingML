Mahadbt Scholarship

Introduction

The Maharashtra Direct Benefit Transfer Scholarship for 2023 is a beneficial scholarship scheme offered by the Maharashtra state government. This webpage provides essential information about the scholarship, including eligibility criteria, required documents, and application procedures.

Details of Mahadbt Scholarship

  • Name: Mahadbt Scholarship 2023
  • Launched by: Maharashtra Government
  • Beneficiaries: Students
  • Objective: Providing Scholarships

Official Website: https://mahadbtmahait.gov.in/

Eligibility Criteria

EBC Department

Financial assistance is provided under this scholarship scheme to students belonging to EBC categories who are pursuing government-approved courses. Check the eligibility criteria for this scheme.

Minority Development Department

Scholarships are offered to minority community students pursuing higher and professional courses. Three types of scholarships are available under this scheme. Verify the eligibility criteria before applying.

Documents Required

  • Income Certificate (Provided by Tahsildar)
  • Cast Certificate
  • Cast Validity Certificate
  • Mark sheet for last appeared examination
  • Mark sheet for SSC or HSC
  • Father date Certificate (if required)
  • Hostel Certificate (if required)
  • CAP round allotment letter
  • Aadhar card
  • Ration card
  • Disability certificate
  • Bank account details
  • Residence proof
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport size photograph
  • Mobile number

Registration Procedure

  1. Visit the Official Website
  2. Click the New Registration button
  3. Enter Aadhaar Number
  4. Click on the "Send OTP" button
  5. Login using your credentials
  6. Choose the Scheme
  7. Fill the Application Form
  8. Upload the documents
  9. Submit the Maha DBT Scholarship Application Form
  10. Take a printout

Applicant Login

  1. Go to the official website of Mahadbt Scholarship
  2. On the homepage, click on the post-matric scholarship link
  3. Now click on the applicant login
  4. On this new page, enter your username, password, and captcha code
  5. Click on the login

Download Guidelines and Rules

  1. Go to the official website of MHA DBT
  2. On the homepage, click on the post-matric scholarship link
  3. Now click on guidelines and rules
  4. A new page will appear before you
  5. On this new page, guidelines and rules will appear before you in PDF format
  6. You have to click on the download option in order to download it
