About Us

Anjuman Islam's Kalsekar Polytechnique is a leading institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical education. We are committed to nurturing young talents and helping them excel in their chosen fields.

Anjuman-I-Islam’s A. R. Kalsekar Polytechnic (AIARKP), established in 2002 is managed by the prestigious Anjuman-I-Islam Trust. The Campus is fast becoming a hi-tech bustling Campus with the introduction of Degree Level Institutions under A.I.’s Kalsekar Technical Campus (AIKTC).

Vision

To be the most sought after Polytechnic that others would wish to emulate.

Mission

Creating Exuberant Technical Professionals.
